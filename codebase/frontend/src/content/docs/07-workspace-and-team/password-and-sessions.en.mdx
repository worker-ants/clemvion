This page explains how to change your password and what happens to your other sessions when you do.

## Changing your password

<ImplAnchor
  kind="ui-entry"
  file="codebase/frontend/src/app/(main)/profile/change-password/page.tsx"
  symbol="ChangePasswordPage"
  describes="Profile → change password page entry point"
/>

Go to [Profile → Change Password](/profile/change-password). Security settings (2FA · Passkey) are managed separately under [Profile → Security](/profile/security).

<Steps>
<li>

Navigate to [Profile → Change Password](/profile/change-password).

</li>
<li>

Enter your **current password**. The change cannot proceed unless this matches your existing password.

</li>
<li>

Enter a **new password** that meets all of the following requirements:

- At least 8 characters
- Contains **3 or more** of the following character types: lowercase letters, uppercase letters, numbers, special characters

</li>
<li>

Re-enter the new password to confirm, then click **Change**.

</li>
</Steps>

Once the change is complete, your current browser session stays active and you are returned to the profile page.

## What happens to other sessions?

When you successfully change your password, **all sessions on other devices are immediately signed out** — only the device you are currently using remains logged in.

You can use a password change to instantly revoke access from a lost device or any suspicious session. Your current browser session is preserved so you can continue working right away.

<Callout type="note">
  To individually terminate specific sessions, visit [Profile → Sessions](/profile/sessions).
</Callout>

## Accounts without a password (OAuth / social login only)

Accounts created exclusively through social login (Google, GitHub, etc.) do not have a password set. Because the current-password verification step cannot be passed, the change-password flow is not available for these accounts.

Setting a password for a social login account is not currently supported.

## Changing your email address

<ImplAnchor
  kind="ui-entry"

  symbol="ChangeEmailPage"
  describes="Profile → change email page entry point"
/>

On the [Profile](/profile) page, click **Change** next to your email address to open the [email change page](/profile/change-email).

<Steps>
<li>

Navigate to [Profile → Change Email](/profile/change-email).

</li>
<li>

Enter your **new email address**.

</li>
<li>

Verify your identity by entering your **current password** or your **2FA code**.

</li>
<li>

Click **Send confirmation email**. A confirmation message will be sent to the new address you entered.

</li>
<li>

Click the link in the confirmation email to finalize the change. The link is only processed in your current logged-in session.

</li>
</Steps>

### Session behaviour after confirmation

Once the email change is confirmed, **all sessions on other devices are immediately signed out** — only your current device remains logged in. This is the same security policy that applies when you change your password.

### Notification sent to your previous email

When the change is confirmed, a notification email is sent to your **previous email address** to inform you of the update. If you did not request this change, use the link in that email to reset your password and secure your account.

### Cancelling or resending while a change is pending

If you did not receive the confirmation email or want to cancel the request, go to the [email change page](/profile/change-email) and choose **Resend** or **Cancel**.

<Callout type="note">
  The confirmation link is valid for **1 hour** after it is sent. If it has expired, resend the email and use the new link.
</Callout>

### When email change is not available

Accounts that use **social login (OAuth) only and have neither a password nor 2FA configured** cannot change their email address because there is no verification method available. To proceed, set a password or enable 2FA first.

<Callout type="tip">
  You can set up 2FA under [Profile → Security](/profile/security).
</Callout>
